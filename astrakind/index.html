What AstraKind Is

AstraKind helps people talk with AI companions such as Bodhi Guru and Pastor Tim, create custom companions, remember meaningful context locally, and use check-ins, widgets, Siri actions, and image prompts.

AstraKind is not a doctor, therapist, lawyer, financial advisor, emergency responder, or real human relationship. It supports reflection and connection while encouraging real-world support when stakes are high.

Safety

If you may hurt yourself, may hurt someone else, or are in immediate danger, contact emergency services now. In the United States, call or text 988 for the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
