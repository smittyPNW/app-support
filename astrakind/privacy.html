AstraKind Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Local-First Design

AstraKind is designed so conversation history, custom companions, relationship profile context, generated chat images, model selections, and first-run disclosure acceptance are stored locally on your device in the current build.

No Developer Data Collection in the Current Build

The current local-only build has no account system, no developer analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no tracking domains, and no developer server collection. The app privacy manifest declares no collected data types and no tracking.

Optional Model Downloads

If you choose to download optional on-device model assets, the app contacts Hugging Face to download model files. Chat text, memories, custom companions, generated images, and relationship profile data are not sent to Hugging Face for those downloads.

Safety and Crisis Boundaries

AstraKind is not a doctor, therapist, lawyer, financial advisor, emergency responder, or real human relationship. If you may hurt yourself, may hurt someone else, or are in immediate danger, contact emergency services now. In the United States, call or text 988 for the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.

Your Choices

You can delete local conversation transcripts and generated chat images with Delete All Chat History in Settings. You can reset the relationship profile separately. Deleting the app removes app data from the device subject to normal iOS behavior.

Contact

Questions about AstraKind privacy can be sent to support@smittyPNW.art.
