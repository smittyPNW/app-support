Frequently asked

How do I get a hint?

Tap the lightbulb button above the checklist — the camera flies to a hidden object and a beacon pulses over its hiding spot.

Can I replay a world?

Yes — tap the restart arrow in a world, or finish everything and choose Start a New Adventure. Every object re-hides in new places.

Does the game need an internet connection?

No. Seek & Spark runs entirely on your device.
