Frequently asked

Why does Shutter Scout ask for my location?

Your location is used on your device to compute light conditions — golden hour, blue hour, sun and moon position — for where you actually are. It never leaves your device.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. Shutter Scout's guidance is computed entirely on your device.
