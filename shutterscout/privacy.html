Privacy Policy

Shutter Scout — effective June 12, 2026

The short version

Shutter Scout collects no data. Your location is used only on your device, only while you use the app.

Location

  • With your permission, the app reads your device location to compute sun, moon, and light conditions for your area.
  • That computation happens entirely on your device. Your location is never stored by us, transmitted to us, or shared with anyone.
  • You can decline or revoke location access anytime in Settings; the app still works with a manually chosen area.

Everything else

  • No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking.
  • No third-party SDKs are included in the app.
  • Your preferences are saved only on your device (and in your personal device backups, which we have no access to).
